Privacy Policy – tastasworld Apps

Effective date: April 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications published by tastasworld. It explains how we handle information in connection with your use of our apps.

Our apps do not directly collect any personal information. We do not require you to create an account or provide your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information.

Some of our apps use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain information to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID)
  • Device information (device type, OS version)
  • IP address

For more information on how Google collects and uses data, please see Google's Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:
Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Our apps may store data (such as game records or settings) locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to any server and is only used within the app.

4. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
